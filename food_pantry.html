Welcome to the Michigan Maize and Blue Cupboard: Food Pantry

The Michigan Maize and Blue Cupboard (MBC) provides a variety of resources to help students meet their basic needs, including access to food, hygiene products, and other support services. The Food Pantry is here to assist students who may be experiencing food insecurity.

Mission Statement: We are committed to providing healthy, nutritious food options to support your academic and personal well-being. No student should have to choose between food and their education.

Groceries

MBC offers a variety of fresh produce, canned goods, and dry foods.

Personal Care Products

Students can access essential hygiene products such as soap, toothpaste, and other necessities.

Kitchen & Cooking Essentials

The pantry also provides kitchenware such as dishes, silverware, pots & pans, Tupperware, and cutting boards.

How to Access the Food Pantry

To access the Food Pantry, you must be a registered University of Michigan student. You can visit the pantry during open hours, and no appointment is necessary. Students are welcome to visit the pantry once a week to pick up food and personal care items.

  • Location: The Food Pantry is located in the basement of the Betsy Barbour Residence Hall, but please enter via the Maynard entrance only to ensure privacy for residents.
  • Address: 420 S State St Ann Arbor, MI 48109
  • Hours of Operation:
    Sunday: 2pm-6pm
    Monday - Thursday: 3pm-7pm
    Friday: 12pm-7pm
    Saturday: closed
  • Eligibility: All University of Michigan students are eligible to use the pantry.
  • Phone: 734-936-2794
